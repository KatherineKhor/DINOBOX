DIE FOR YOU
The WEEKND





"Die for You" is a song by Canadian singer The Weeknd, released in 2016 as a part of his third studio album, "Starboy." The song was produced by Doc McKinney, Cirkut, and The Weeknd himself, and co-written by the artist and several other songwriters, including Ed Sheeran.

"Die for You" is a slow-burning R&B ballad that showcases The Weeknd's signature falsetto vocals. The song features atmospheric synths and a minimal beat, which allow the vocals to take center stage. Lyrically, the song is about a toxic and obsessive love that the narrator is willing to do anything for, including dying for the person.

The chorus of "Die for You" features some of the most memorable lyrics in the song: "And I don't want to see you with him / 'Cause I don't get sleep at night / And I don't need to hear you tell me / That I'm alright." These lyrics convey the narrator's desperation and his unwillingness to let go of his lover, despite the damage it is causing him.

Overall, "Die for You" is a hauntingly beautiful R&B ballad that showcases The Weeknd's vocal prowess and songwriting skills. The song has resonated with fans, and has become a fan-favorite from the "Starboy" album.
