"2 soon" is a song by Keshi that was released in 2019. It is a slow and introspective ballad that explores the pain and heartbreak of losing someone too soon. The song features Keshi's signature dreamy and atmospheric sound, with his soothing vocals floating over a bed of lush synths and subtle guitar riffs.

Lyrically, "2 soon" is a poignant meditation on loss and grief. Keshi sings about the pain of losing someone too soon and struggling to move on from the memories they left behind. He expresses his longing to be with the person he lost, singing "And I know you're gone, but I still wish you were here with me."

Overall, "2 soon" is a beautiful and emotional song that showcases Keshi's talent for writing deeply introspective and relatable music. The song has resonated with fans around the world and has become a fan-favorite in Keshi's discography.
