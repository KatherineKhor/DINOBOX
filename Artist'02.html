"Instagram" is a song by South Korean R&B singer-songwriter DEAN, released in 2017 as part of his debut EP, "130 Mood: TRBL." The song was co-written and co-produced by DEAN himself, and features rapper Crush.

"Instagram" is a smooth and groovy R&B track that captures the bittersweet feelings of loneliness and longing that can come with social media usage. The song features a laid-back beat, warm synths, and DEAN's signature falsetto vocals, as well as a verse from Crush that complements DEAN's vocals perfectly.

Lyrically, "Instagram" is a commentary on how social media can create a sense of distance and disconnection between people, despite being designed to bring them closer together. The song expresses the feelings of loneliness and isolation that can come from constantly scrolling through social media and comparing oneself to others. The chorus, in particular, captures this sentiment: "I don't wanna be alone / But I'm always alone / On my phone, on my Instagram."

Overall, "Instagram" is a poignant and introspective R&B track that reflects on the darker side of social media and the impact it can have on our mental health and relationships. The song has resonated with fans around the world and has cemented DEAN's reputation as one of the most talented and insightful R&B artists of his generation.
